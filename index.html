Micro Architecture Simulator Suite

Welcome to MASS

MASS (Micro Architecture Simulator Suite) provides a simple yet powerful environment for exploring processor microarchitecture. It features an interactive RISC-V Pipeline Simulator and a detailed Cache Simulator, allowing step-by-step visualization of instruction flow, hazards, stalls, and memory access behavior. Designed for students, educators, and researchers, the suite makes Computer Architecture concepts intuitive and engaging.
Click the Launch buttons to access the simulators and engage in interactive learning, teaching, and experimentation.
